# Pinout ESP32

Name          | GPIO | Used by
:-------------|-----:|:---------
`SDA`         | `23` | BME280
`SCL`         | `22` | BME280

`SCK`         | `5`  | PCA301
`MISO`        | `19` | PCA301
`MOSI`        | `18` | PCA301

`A1`          | `25` | PCA301 Interrupt
`A5`          | `4`  | PCA301 Slave Select

`A6`          | `14` | Hygrometer VCC
`A7`          | `32` | Hygrometer ADC

`A8`          | `5`  | DHT22

`A9`          | `33` | RC Switch

`BUILTIN_LED` | `13` | LED
